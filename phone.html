ABOUT MOBILE PHONE

Mobile phone is a portable telephone set. We can move it easily from place to place. It can make and receive calls using wireless media. It is used to communicate with any person in the furthest place. Mobile phone converts our voice into an electrical signal. Then the electrical signal is transmitted to the destination using radio waves and converted into sound to the target phone. Currently, mobile phones are not just used to make phone calls. It has already brought a revolution in the communication system in our society. Now it provides a variety of other services, such as text messaging, MMS, email, Internet access, video games, digital photography, etc. Mobile communication is very much important to the businessman and high officials. But, it is no less important to other professionals and common people. There are a few drawbacks of using mobile phone for long hours. It causes cancer, increases stress, hinders sleep, and hampers thinking power, and so on. Young generation often becomes mislead by using mobile phone. Criminals carry out terrorist activities using mobile phones. Despite all demerits, mobile phone is considered the blessings of modren science.
